How to Update Links
  1. Click Settings (top row) and paste your GitHub token. If you don't have one, ask Tim. Do this once per computer.
  2. Click Editing to turn it ON.
  3. Find the teacher/course (use By Subject, By Cohort, or Simple List).
  4. Click Edit next to the teacher and paste the Google Slides URL. This edits the URL only.
  5. Click Save to GitHub. Wait for “Saved!”, then refresh the page after about a minute.
  6. Optional: Click Export JSON to download a backup of all links.

If a preview looks blank, click Open in new tab above the viewer.
